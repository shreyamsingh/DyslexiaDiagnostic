Dyslexia

Dyslexia is the most common learning disabilities. Approximately 1 in 5 people will experience dyslexia or a form of dyslexia.

Children with dyslexia may experience difficulty reading, problems spelling or sounding out words, and many more.

Dyscalculia

Dyscalculia is characterized by having trouble with basic mathematics and counting.

As children age, their inherent mathematical abilities change, so signs of discalculia change as well.
